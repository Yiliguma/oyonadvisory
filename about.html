About

Oyon Advisory was founded to bring greater clarity to deep-tech questions that sit between research, industry, and investment.

We work across the boundaries of academia and market, combining field research, analysis, writing, and independent perspective. Our approach is thoughtful, flexible, and grounded in the belief that complex technologies deserve clear and careful interpretation.

Founded by Yili, Oyon Advisory reflects a background spanning scientific research, deep-tech investing, and cross-border market analysis. The practice exists to help clients understand emerging technologies more clearly and make better-informed decisions.

Founder

Yili is the founder of Oyon Advisory. She brings experience across scientific research, deep-tech investing, and cross-border market analysis.
