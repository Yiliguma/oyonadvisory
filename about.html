About

Oyon Advisory is an independent research initiative focused on deep technology, industrial systems, and investment.

It is a place for examining how complex technologies move from research into companies and markets—and for testing clearer ways to evaluate the evidence, risks, and institutions around them.

Current work includes written research and the experimental Oyon DeepTech Readiness Scorecard.
