Oyon DeepTech Readiness Scorecard

An AI-assisted technical screening concept for investors evaluating materials, semiconductor, energy, and industrial science startups.

The demo focuses on scoring logic: technical validity, evidence quality, scale-up feasibility, market pull, defensibility, and capital efficiency.

Demo version: this page shows a simulated Oyon DeepTech Readiness Scorecard. It does not process confidential materials or connect to a live AI model yet.

Technical judgment

Designed to surface the questions that matter before a first founder call, rather than producing a generic investment memo.

Stage adjusted

Pre-seed, seed, Series A, and Series B+ companies require different evidence thresholds and diligence expectations.

Evidence aware

The score is paired with confidence, missing evidence, and investor follow-up questions to avoid false precision.
